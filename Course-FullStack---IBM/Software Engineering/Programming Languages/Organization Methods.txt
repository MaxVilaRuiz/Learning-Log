Flowcharts:
- Graphical / Pictorial representation of an algorithm.
- The hierarchy of colors, symbols and other elements is used to mark the program process.
- Connectors: arrow; Data (input/output): parallelogram; Decision: diamond; Process (operations): rectangle; Start/End: circle.
- Flowcharts softwares: Microsoft Visio, Lucidchart, Draw.io, DrawAnyWhere.
